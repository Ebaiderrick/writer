Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

EyaWriter collects the minimum information needed to provide the service: your email address and display name (via Firebase Authentication), and the screenplay projects you save to cloud storage (via Firestore).

How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the EyaWriter service — syncing projects, enabling collaboration, and authenticating your account. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Firebase & third-party services

EyaWriter uses Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage. Projects saved to the cloud are stored in Firestore under your user account. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information about how Firebase handles data.

AI features

When you use AI writing features, the text of the current screenplay block and recent story context are sent to an AI provider (OpenRouter / OpenAI) to generate suggestions. No personal identifying information is included in these requests. See our AI Usage Disclosure for details.

Local storage

EyaWriter stores application preferences (theme, language, editor settings) in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Contact

For privacy enquiries, please contact us through the repository at github.com/Ebaiderrick/writer.

AI Usage Disclosure

Last updated: May 2026

What gets sent to the AI

When you use an AI writing action (Expand, Rephrase, Predict, etc.), EyaWriter sends the following to the AI provider:

  • The text of the current screenplay block
  • Recent story context (up to the last few scenes)
  • The selected action type and any custom instruction you typed

No account information, email address, or other personal data is included in AI requests.

AI provider

By default, EyaWriter routes AI requests through OpenRouter, which provides access to multiple language models (including OpenAI's GPT series). You can configure a custom endpoint in Settings → AI to use any compatible API.

Data retention

EyaWriter does not store AI request or response data. Requests are processed in real-time and the response is returned directly to your editor. Refer to the AI provider's privacy policy for their data handling practices.

Your control

AI features are opt-in and require enabling "AI Assistance" in the editor toolbar. You can disable AI assistance at any time. No AI processing occurs unless you explicitly trigger an AI action.
