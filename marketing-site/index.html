🧠 Story Memory

Your story's
living database.

Story Memory automatically tracks every character, location, scene, theme, and plot point in your script. Add your own entries — with character age, look, behaviour, backstory — and EyaWriter injects that context into every AI request, keeping the AI consistent with your world.

Never lose continuity. If a character changes their hair colour in Act 1, the AI knows by Act 3.

✦ FAQ

Questions answered

Yes. Core features are genuinely free forever — no credit card, no time limit. You get unlimited scripts, cloud sync, AI assistance (rate-limited shared endpoint), real-time collaboration, Story Memory, and DOCX export at no cost. PDF export, priority AI, and team workspaces are the paid Pro tier, coming soon.
Every AI request automatically includes your Story Memory as context — characters (with age, look, behaviour, backstory), locations, themes, and plot points. The AI is instructed to respect all these elements for consistency. The richer your Story Memory, the more accurate and personalised the AI output.
EyaWriter imports .fountain (used by Final Draft, Arc Studio, Fade In, WriterDuet), .txt (plain text screenplay), .md (Markdown), and .json (EyaWriter project backup). Scenes, dialogue, and action lines are parsed automatically. Export is available as TXT, DOCX, PDF, and JSON.
Invite collaborators by email. They receive an invitation to join your script and can accept or decline. Once accepted, you both see each other's presence in the workspace awareness panel. Edits sync in real time via Firebase Firestore. Up to 5 collaborators per project on the free tier. Inline comments thread on specific lines with @mention support.
Your scripts are stored in Firestore with strict per-user access rules. Only you and collaborators you explicitly invite can access your work. We do not train AI models on your scripts, and your content is never shared with third parties.
