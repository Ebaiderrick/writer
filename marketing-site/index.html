✦ FAQ

Questions answered

Yes — core features are genuinely free, forever. No credit card, no time limit. You get unlimited scripts, cloud sync, AI assistance, real-time collaboration, and DOCX export at no cost. Pro features like PDF export and priority AI are in development for the paid tier.
The AI assistant is context-aware and trained on screenplay conventions. It can suggest dialogue rewrites, check character voice consistency, propose scene structure improvements, and even help with proofreading. Free tier users share an AI endpoint with rate limiting; Pro gets priority dedicated processing.
Yes. Real-time collaboration is free. You can invite co-writers with different permission levels (read, comment, edit), see live cursors, leave inline notes, and track changes. Powered by Firebase, so sync is near-instant globally.
DOCX export is available for free, producing a properly formatted Word document. PDF export with industry-standard margins and typography is coming in Pro. Professional script coverage (AI-generated notes) is also a Pro feature in development.
Your scripts are stored in Firestore with strict per-user access rules. Only you and the collaborators you explicitly invite can access your work. We do not train AI models on your content, and your data is never shared with third parties.
