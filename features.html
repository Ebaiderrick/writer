Screenplay Formatting

Industry-standard format, automatically

Stop fighting your word processor. EyaWriter knows screenplay format and applies it as you type — proper margins, fonts, spacing, and element types out of the box.

  • Scene headings (INT./EXT.) with auto-complete
  • Action lines with correct margins
  • Character names and dialogue blocks
  • Parentheticals and dual dialogue
  • Transitions (CUT TO:, FADE OUT.)
  • Act breaks for TV formats
  • Automatic scene numbering
  • Page count tracking
📝
AI Writing Tools

AI that understands screenplay structure

Our AI tools are context-aware. They know whether you're editing a dialogue line or an action block — and respond accordingly. No generic rewrites that break format.

  • Grammar & style correction
  • Dialogue improvement & sharpening
  • Scene improvement suggestions
  • Smart proofreading across the full script
  • Scene generation from a prompt
  • Custom instructions (tone, genre, voice)
  • Bring your own API key — unlimited use
  • AI response streaming for instant feedback

Collaboration

Write together, in real time

Screenwriting is collaborative by nature. EyaWriter makes it easy to bring in co-writers, editors, and directors — all working on the same document simultaneously.

  • Invite collaborators by email
  • Role-based access: Owner, Editor, Viewer
  • Live cursor and real-time sync
  • Threaded comments on any line
  • Resolve and archive comments
  • Shared project workspace
  • Task management with assignments
  • Collaboration activity log

Export

Submission-ready output

When your script is done, EyaWriter exports it exactly as industry readers expect — proper Courier 12pt, 1.5-inch left margin, numbered pages, title page.

  • DOCX export (Word-compatible)
  • PDF export (Pro — coming soon)
  • Proper screenplay margins & fonts
  • Title page with author & contact info
  • Scene numbers in export
  • Selective scene export

Story Memory

Keep track of everything

A 120-page script has hundreds of details to track. EyaWriter's Story Memory captures characters, locations, and key story elements automatically as you write.

  • Automatic character extraction
  • Location registry
  • Scene timeline view
  • Character note cards
  • Search across all scenes
  • Story notes & mood board
